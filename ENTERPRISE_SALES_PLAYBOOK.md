**Objective**: Identify and qualify enterprise prospects

**Activities:**

- **Authority**: CISO, Compliance Director, or VP Engineering involvement
- **Need**: Regulatory compliance pressure or crypto visibility gaps
- **Timeline**: Implementation timeline within 12 months
- **Technical Fit**: Cloud infrastructure, containerized applications

**Success Metrics:**
- 50+ qualified leads per month
- 20% lead-to-opportunity conversion rate
- Average qualification time: 1 week

#### Stage 2: **Discovery & Needs Assessment** (Weeks 3-4)
**Objective**: Deep-dive into customer requirements and pain points

**Key Discovery Questions:**
1. **Regulatory Landscape**: "What compliance frameworks are you required to meet?"
2. **Current Process**: "How do you currently manage crypto asset inventory?"
3. **Pain Points**: "What's the biggest challenge in your compliance program?"
4. **Timeline**: "When do you need to demonstrate compliance?"
5. **Budget Process**: "What's your budget approval process for security tools?"
6. **Technical Environment**: "What's your current infrastructure architecture?"
7. **Decision Process**: "Who else is involved in this decision?"

**Deliverables:**
- Detailed needs assessment document
- Initial ROI calculation
- Technical architecture review
- Stakeholder mapping

#### Stage 3: **Solution Design & Proposal** (Weeks 5-6)
**Objective**: Create customized solution proposal

**Activities:**
- Technical architecture workshop (2-4 hours)
- Solution design document creation
- ROI model customization
- Proposal presentation (executive + technical audiences)

**Proposal Components:**
- Executive summary with business case
- Technical solution architecture
- Implementation timeline and milestones
- Pricing and contract terms
- Risk mitigation plan
- Success criteria and KPIs

#### Stage 4: **Proof of Concept (POC)** (Weeks 7-12)
**Objective**: Demonstrate value with customer's actual data

**POC Scope:**
- Limited production deployment (30-90 days)
- 1-2 key use cases demonstration  
- Representative data set scanning
- Integration with 1-2 existing systems
- Compliance report generation

**Success Criteria:**
- Successful crypto asset discovery (>95% accuracy)
- Compliance report generation for 1 framework
- Performance benchmarks met (<100ms response time)
- User acceptance by security and compliance teams
- Executive sponsor satisfaction

#### Stage 5: **Negotiation & Closing** (Weeks 13-16)
**Objective**: Finalize contract terms and secure signature

**Key Negotiation Areas:**
- Pricing and payment terms
- Data residency and security requirements
- Support SLA and escalation procedures
- Professional services scope and timeline
- Contract length and renewal terms
- Termination and data portability clauses

**Closing Techniques:**
- Urgency creation (regulatory deadlines, budget cycles)
- Risk mitigation (pilot success, reference customers)
- Value reinforcement (ROI calculation, competitive analysis)
- Executive sponsorship (C-level champion engagement)

#### Stage 6: **Implementation & Onboarding** (Weeks 17-28)
**Objective**: Successful production deployment and user adoption

**Implementation Phases:**
1. **Technical Setup** (Weeks 17-20): Infrastructure deployment, integrations
2. **Data Migration** (Weeks 21-22): Historical crypto asset import
3. **User Training** (Weeks 23-24): Team onboarding and enablement  
4. **Go-Live** (Week 25): Production deployment with monitoring
5. **Optimization** (Weeks 26-28): Performance tuning, additional integrations
